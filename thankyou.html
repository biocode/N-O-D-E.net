THANK YOU (+ GIVEAWAY)
--

The end of the year is fast approaching, and it's made me think about 2017 and everything that's happened on this channel.

- Youtube link

Firstly, to be able to work on NODE full time now is a dream for me, and I want you to know that I don't take this for granted, not for a second. This job is the absolute most perfect fit for me, and I feel a moral obligation to not waste this chance.

I appreciate every one of you who has watched or shared the videos, donated, became a patron, or bought something from the shop.

It's been a lot of hard work, but I want to work even harder from now on. In a deeper sense, I want to justify my very existence by doing more, and I promise you that I won't stop until I drop dead.

I also want to say that I feel humbled by you all too. Every time I've asked for help on something, literally hundreds of you stepped forward, and that's a testament to what a great group of people you are.

So in the coming year, I plan on turning everything up to 11. I've been working on some new video series for a while, many new hardware projects, and I have other bigger plans too, but you will find out about all that in time.

Thank you.
